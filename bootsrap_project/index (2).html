How do I decorate my home decor?

According to the top interior designers of India, decor your home interiors with colors of your choice such as bold, bright, neutral or serene, just make sure they are complementary. Also always eensure that your furnishings and accessories complement your walls, decor, flooring, and floor coverings. There should be a balance between style and color so that the space is visually appealing regardless of how it is decorated.
